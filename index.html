Litepaper

Introduction

DeFi Queens is an NFT metaverse game built on the Solana blockchain, it will combine elements of play-to-earn and DeFi. We believe that gaming is the future of entertainment and it has proven to be so over the years while blcokchain gaming is the future of all gaming ecosystem. Currently gaming industry is 200 billion and is perfectly set for disruption.

In addition to gaming blockchain technologies are poised to disrupt traditional finances and this allows for creation of unique products gamified finance set in metaverse. Simplifying many complex financial and DeFi concepts and introducing them though game making them accessible to larger audience will be the future. We can see that this novelty has a strong attraction to gaming community and younger generation and this will be a true disruption to financial and gaming industries changing and revolutionizing both industries. What was once unthinkable is now possible merging games and personal finances, we are truly on the edge of a new world.

In DeFi Queens, players will be able to collect Suits by hunting and seducing them and later converting to slave simps(NFT’s) for PVE or warrior simps(NFT’s) PVP tasks. In PVE environment players will be able complete quests with slave simps to earn DFQ(i.e. stacking mechanism) and generate extra yield depending on there Total Simps Locked value. While in PVP games players will be able to lead there warrior simps to battle with other Queens and raid there lands for loot and resources. By playing the game, players will automatically participate in our player based economy by collecting tokens as rewards for completing tasks, winning battles, and progressing in there quests and missions. Players will also be able to spend there DFQ to upgrade there Queens and simps improving there PVE or PVP capabilities as well as crafting new items, upgrading characters and acquiring new traits.

Main cryptocurrency behind DeFi Queens will be $DFQ. This will be main game coin used for both governance and as an in game reward token. Players will often have a choice how to use there Queens whether they want to acquire more $DFQ or collect additional simps to fawn to there Queens.

We plan to incorporate original concepts of DeFi such as liquidity mining and bonding in game to produce protocol owned liquidity this will allow the game to pay out highly competitive $DFQ rewards to gamers and insure healthy tokenomics for a lasting project. By not being greedy and diverting most of the raised liquidity back to games ecosystem we will create a fair ecosystem for gamers around the world. We believe players should own their own assets and have full custody over what happens to them. We believe that players should be able to participate in an open marketplace whereby any value they contribute to the ecosystem should be rewarded just like any real world economy.
